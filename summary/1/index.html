The Core Challenge: Key Disadvantages for MRT Users

To build a future-ready transit system, we must first understand the primary pain points affecting riders today. From the moment they leave their homes to the time they reach their destination, users face significant hurdles that impact convenience, comfort, and confidence in the MRT system.

Blueprint for a Future-Ready MRT

A strategic, multi-layered approach is required to transform user disadvantages into opportunities for innovation. This roadmap outlines key initiatives to build a more efficient, accessible, and user-centric system.

1

Digital Service Excellence

Immediately redevelop the official MRT app with a focus on stability, real-time data, and offline access.

2

Integrated Micro-Mobility

Launch AI-driven on-demand feeder services and bike/scooter sharing integrated into the main app.

3

Smart Operations

Deploy AI for crowd management to dynamically adjust train frequency and implement IoT for predictive maintenance.

4

Urban Integration & Accessibility

Champion Transit-Oriented Development with urban planners and accelerate universal accessibility upgrades through Public-Private Partnerships.
