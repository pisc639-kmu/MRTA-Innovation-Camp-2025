The Challenge of Overcrowding

Overcrowding is a significant deterrent to public transit adoption, impacting user comfort, safety, and overall system efficiency. During peak hours, the Bangkok MRT faces substantial challenges that diminish the rider experience. Understanding the root causes and implementing smart solutions are crucial for a future-ready system.

Understanding the Bottlenecks

Overcrowding isn't just about too many people; it's a complex issue stemming from outdated infrastructure, inefficient design, and a lack of dynamic management.
